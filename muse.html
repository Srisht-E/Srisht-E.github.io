MUSE

Deepika,Karishma,Swaathi Guided by Prof.Unais Sait

Muse is device that allows any music lover to explore and create music. While other musical instruments requires one to learn to create music in a structured manner, Muse allows everyone to be free and play with it to create music. It creates a sense of excitement as the user plays around with it to create different patterns of notes. As different notes play, different led’s light up, amusing and engaging the user.

It gives the user a better understanding of music as different led’s light up as different notes play. The led lights also fascinates the user.

Product Design, Mechatronics, Branding, Music
